WELCOME TO THE HALO WARS 2 COMMANDER APP

Commander App is your personal Halo Wars 2 companion and provides you with the best detailed information on your games for you to improve your skils. Get crucial insights on every match and find out where you shine and where you need to improve on. The app is currently in BETA phase and supports only Halo Wars 2 on the 1v1 Ranked Playlist. More gamemodes can be considered for the future such as Blitz and Skirmish.

Commander App's Features:

Paginated List of all your 1v1 matches.

Type in your gamertag to see all your 1v1 matches.

You can also take a look at other people's games!

Summary of each match.

Data includes map, leaders, victors, total units built/lost/destroyed by unit type.

You can see which of your units never survive or which units you had trouble getting rid of!

Detailed performance of each match in the form of Battles.

Battles are determined by the amount of units that died in a linked period of time.

Battles can be classified by small, medium or large.

Filter out battles by size using the controls at the top of the Battles tab.

You can also remove specific battles by clicking the Remove button on its card.

Each Battle contains detailed information on costs, units and kills.

Battles show your current Battle as well as reinforcements (units built in during the duration of a battle).

You can see which player won each battle and see how much resources your army cost as well as how much resources you lost.

Check detailed information on each unit.

You can see individual costs, as well as indicators for Living and Dead units.

By clicking a specific unit of interest you can see which units killed it.

Multi-platform support (PC, Xbox).
