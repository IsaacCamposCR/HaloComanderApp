HOW DO I USE THE APP?

Here you can see an image of the application with several Points of Interest detailing every component of the application, hover over the small red circles with a cross inside to display tooltips on what each component does.



  • More

    ENTER YOUR GAMERTAG

    Type your Xbox Live gamertag in the text field and click GO to look at your recent games.

    Close
  • More

    MATCH HISTORY

    Once you enter your gamertag you will find a list of your 10 most recent games.

    Close
  • More

    PAGE CONTROLS

    Use the forward or backwards buttons to retrieve the next 10 games (older) or the previous 10 games (most recent).

    Close
  • More

    SELECTED MATCH

    You can select a match (light blue) to display detailed information about it in the right panel.

    Close
  • More

    STATISTICS TAB

    Click on the statistics tab to obtain a summary of the match including: map, leaders, victors, total units built/lost/destroyed by unit type.

    Close
  • More

    BATTLES TAB

    Click on the battles tab to obtain detailed information on each battle that ocurred during the game.

    Close
  • More

    FILTER CONTROLS

    Use the switches to display only the battles you're most interested in. You can filter by size.

    Close
  • More

    BATTLE INFO CARD

    This is the battle info card. A single game can contain many battle cards with very detailed information. Such as who won said battle, army composition, reinforcements (if any) and much more.

    Close
  • More

    ARMY COST

    Here you can see the Supply, Power and Population Cost for your army (black), as well as how many resources you lost (red). The player with the highest population at the end of the battle is declared the winner.

    Close
  • More

    ARMY COMPOSITION

    Units that compose an army at a given time are displayed individually, and you can compare it with your enemy's. For example you can see the army was composed of four grunts and a chopper, three of them were killed.

    Close
  • More

    REINFORCEMENTS COMPOSITION

    Aditionally, you can see both player's reinforcements. Units built during the battle or dropped by leader powers are shown here.

    Close
  • More

    OUTCOME

    The bottom portion of the Battle Info Card shows the outcome of the battle, detailing the size of the battle, the game time it ocurred on, amount of units killed and of course each of the units killed.

    Close
  • More

    REMOVE CARD

    Click this button when you don't want to display a certain card anymore. Don't worry, reselecting a match brings it back!

    Close
  • More

    UNIT DETAILS

    At any time in any of the panels you can click a specific unit to obtain detailed data about it such as costs and which enemy units contributed to the unit getting killed.
